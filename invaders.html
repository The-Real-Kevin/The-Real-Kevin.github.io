Play Space Invaders

A classic arcade game recreation built with Construct 3. Defend Earth from the alien invasion!

How to Play

  • Arrow Keys (← →) - Move your ship left and right
  • Spacebar - Fire lasers at the invaders
  • Goal - Destroy all aliens before they reach the bottom!
