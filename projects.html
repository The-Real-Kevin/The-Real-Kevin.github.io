Recycle Royale (2022 December - 2023 December)

Project lead for Recycle Royale, a system of smart bins that uses machine vision to sort recyclable trash from landfill. Users are encouraged to recycle through a gamified app that rewards them with points for each item recycled. The points can be redeemed for in-game cosmetics, prizes or donated to charity. Check out Recycle Royale app on the App Store page and the website for more!
