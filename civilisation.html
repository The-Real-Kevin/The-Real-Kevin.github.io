About This Game

Civilisation is an open-world strategy game where you manage a society through various challenges. Complete missions, interact with NPCs, and make decisions that shape your civilization's future.

Controls:

  • Arrow Keys - Move your character
  • E Key - Interact with objects, NPCs, and enter buildings
  • F Key - Use items
  • R Key - Additional actions
  • M Key - View instructions
  • C Key - View credits
